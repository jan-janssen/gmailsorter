Gmailsorter Welcome to Gmailsorter your automated email labeling solution for Google Mail. Starting from your existing email labels Gmailsorter calculates the similarity of incoming emails and then assigns email labels accordingly. So you no longer need to specify your email filters manually.




Caution: For mailsort to label your emails, it needs access to your Google Mail account. If you have existing email filters configured in Google Mail, disable them before signing in to Gmailsorter.
